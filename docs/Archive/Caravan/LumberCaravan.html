Pay rates

1gp per day per person, doubled if.

Cargo

1sp per kg lumber.
20pp per 2 ton wagon.
Ten wagons is 200pp|2000gp minus wages.

Personnel

Caravan Master and assistant with supply wagon. 3gp per wagon in caravan, plus 2gp bonus
Wagoneer and apprentice per cargo wagon. 1 gp per pair
Two guards per cargo wagon. 2 gp per pair, 4 if bonus. 2gp fine for falling asleep on duty, third instance is dismissal.
Scout per two wagons, 5sp per one, 15sp if bonus
Bonus requires arrive on time with full cargo

32.5|57.5 per day and 10-12 days journey
10 days = 575 if full, otherwise 390 (~12 days)
11 days = 357.5
12 days = 390
More than 12 days only paid if exceptional circumstances / beyond control

Duties

Rotating shifts for guards, drivers and scouts.
Guards and drivers share cooking duties
In event of attack guards engage, drivers either keep driving while assistant uses longbow, or both on longbows if no chance of escape, scouts harass from distance/behind. One scout designated to ride ahead to nearest settlement to record attack.

First half

Heading north from Mesmolignes via Conlansoy to the south-east petran overland trail til it hits the river, where the cargo is loaded on to barges. Drivers and wagons return or head elsewhere, while the guards, scouts, and carvan master continue.

Rumors / World Events

The overland trip briefly passed through two hunting towns, each approximately 4000 residents. In Villontarmes (vil ont arms) and Chaushercot (chow sher cot) the caravan manages to pick up rumors some of the nobility and rich have been in a foul mood, locals suspecting the Black Watch must have made ground against the Crushed Velvet Society.

4-7 minor bandit attack. Group check of atk roll vs DC14, if fail then fight is won but wounds and lose a day
8-9 thief in night. Group check of perception vs DC13, if fail then some cargo stolen
10-18 Uneventful days
19 Make good journey time and claw back a day
20 Scouts manage to spot thieves trailing caravan and recover cargo OR make good journey time and claw back a day

Third Quarter

The cargo spread across three barges, two taking four loads and the third carrying two loads, the scout horses and caravan master’s wagon (wheels removed).
Ship 1 has a captain, and three crewmen. 8 guards, 1 scout look-out
Ship 2 has a captain, and three crewmen. 5 guards, 3 party guards, 1 scout look-out, Haggy
Ship 3 has a captain, and three crewmen. 4 guards, 1 scout look-out, 2 scouts tending horses, caravan master and assistant
2gp passage for Big D, Nyska and Wagon (wheels removed) on ship 3. Free passage for Haggy, with expectation she fights if trouble occurs.

2 days broken into 6am - 6pm & 6pm - 12am ⇒ four rolls against table.

Rumors / World Events

In Sulleaulegar (sue lou le gar) they reiterate the rumors about the CVS and add the head of a noble family has stepped down and given his estates to his nephew, suggestions being they were caught up in something.

  • Some of the more devout looking fellows seem happy. Having heard a squad Knights of Helm dispatched the assassin killing off clergy.

Last Quarter

Con save DC 13 when disembark, as the two days of particularly spicy petran food plus being on the water may cause some digestion issues and poor sleep. Stage one exhaustion on fail. After each long rest can re-roll save, each subsequent day the DC drops by 1.

Scout horses unloaded, scouts head out, cargo loaded onto fresh wagons, transported wagons reassembled. Caravan resumes journey to Orle.

3-4 days ⇒ three rolls against table, four if experience delays

Rumors / World Events

About half an hour out from Orle, passing traffic in the other direction, the caravan grinds to a halt as a rumor ripples quickly from wagon to wagon. Allegedly the temple of Helm in Grielle has been desecrated and the ranking Clergy (Primate, Lord Protector and High Protector) are dead. ** A visiting deputy from Filnilauiy was also kill in the temple.
